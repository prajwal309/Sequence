Rules of the Game

The Sequence game rules are simple:

  • Each player takes turns playing a card and placing a marker on the board.
  • Jacks have special powers: One-eyed Jacks remove markers, and two-eyed Jacks act as wild cards.
  • The goal is to form a sequence of five markers in a row, column, or diagonal.
